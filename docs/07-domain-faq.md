# 07 - Domain FAQ (Scientific Skincare + Trend)

## Q1. What is "scientific" in this project?
Recommendation must be grounded in ingredient efficacy, suitability, contraindications, and compliance constraints.

## Q2. What is "trend" in this project?
Trend is an actionable signal, not a keyword alone. It combines growth, cross-platform consistency, persistence, and new-product relevance.

## Q3. When trend conflicts with science?
Science/safety wins as hard constraint; trend is optimization only.

## Q4. Why not use full user profile?
MVP uses anonymized profile buckets for compliance and delivery speed.

## Q5. What qualifies as safe output?
No prohibited claims, no high-risk combinations, includes clear warning/disclaimer when needed.

## Q6. What is the minimum acceptable answer format?
- recommendation
- scientific basis
- trend basis
- safety warning
- disclaimer
